The Curator - Hrushikesh Magar

Interior design is the art and science of enhancing the interior of a building to achieve a healthier and more aesthetically pleasing environment for the people using the space. It involves a variety of tasks, including planning the layout of a space, choosing and arranging furniture and decorative elements, selecting colors and materials, and working with architects and contractors to ensure that the space meets the functional and aesthetic needs of the client.

Interior designers work in a range of settings, including residential homes, commercial spaces, and public buildings such as hospitals and schools. They may specialize in a particular type of design, such as sustainable design or hospitality design, and may work for design firms, architecture firms, or as freelance designers.

Specterior Designs is derived from the initials of the names of principal designers Hrushikesh Magar , one of India's top interior designers. Specterior Designs' brand signifies the importance that the interior design firm places on using lighting to create the right interiors and ambience, be it day or night. Hrushikesh Magar are Interior Designer from Suryadatta University of Interior Design, a well-known designer with an in-depth knowledge of lighting, established Spacterior Designs in 2015. Ever since, they have been committed to delivering a timeless and fresh aesthetic to several high-scale residential, commercial and development builder projects across India. Drawing on their combined experience of 5+ years, the duo can be trusted to create design magic by skilfully experimenting with space, colours, materials and lights.
