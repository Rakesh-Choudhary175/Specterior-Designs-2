OUR FANTASTIC TEAM

The Curator - Hrushikesh Magar

Founder and Principal Designer, Leena Deshpande Desai is a graduate from the B N college of Architecture, Pune. She pursued her post graduation degree in Spatial Design from the Nanyang academy of fine arts (Singapore) as well as from the University of Huddersfield (UK).Her passion for design stems from her love for spaces and visual aesthetics.

Her innovative ideas and expressive thinking show in her creative designs. After commencing her journey as a Design coordinator with various globally acclaimed firms in India as well as Singapore, she pursued a graduate program in Construction project management at NICMAR, Pune. Another feather in her hat is the Goldman Sachs scholarship (ISB certified) she earned for Leadership and Management

Her vision encompasses imparting impeccable life forms to the design.
